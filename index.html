Marvel's Cinematic Universe

Marvel's Cinematic Universe involves an entire media project created by Marvel Studies that includes movies, TV shows, comics, and short films.
The main focus are the superhero films.

Marvel Studios divided the superhero films in three stages. As of today (5/15/2016), we are on the first movie of the third phase.
